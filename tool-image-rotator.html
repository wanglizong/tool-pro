How to Rotate or Flip Your Image

  1. Upload an Image: Drag your file to the upload box or click to select it from your device.
  2. Rotate or Flip: Use the control buttons to rotate the image left or right, or to flip it horizontally or vertically. The preview will update instantly.
  3. Download Your Image: Once you are satisfied with the result, click the "Download Image" button to save your new, permanently transformed image.
