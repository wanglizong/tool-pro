How to Use

FAQs

  • Q: Are eSignatures legally valid? Yes, electronic signatures are legally binding in many countries, often compliant with regulations like eIDAS (EU) & UETA/ESIGN Act (US). However, always verify specific legal requirements for your use case. This demo simulates the process.
  • Q: Can I sign multiple pages or add other fields like date? This basic demo focuses on a single signature. Full-featured eSign tools allow multiple signatures, initials, date fields, and text boxes across various pages.
  • Q: Is my data secure? Security is paramount for eSignatures. Real eSign services use encryption and secure storage. This demo is client-side and doesn't store your files long-term.
  • Q: What signature image formats can I upload? PNG and JPEG are commonly supported. Transparent PNGs are ideal for signatures.
