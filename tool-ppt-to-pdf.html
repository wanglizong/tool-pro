Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my uploaded files secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. The entire conversion process is automated and secure. We permanently delete all files from our servers shortly after conversion, ensuring your data remains confidential.

Will the PDF look the same as my PowerPoint?

Yes, our conversion tool is designed to maintain the highest fidelity. It preserves the layout, formatting, fonts, and images of your original PowerPoint presentation, so your PDF will look just as you designed it.

What PowerPoint versions are supported?

Our converter supports both the classic .ppt format from older versions of PowerPoint and the modern .pptx format (Office Open XML). You can confidently upload files from any version of PowerPoint.
