Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not a feature, it's our foundation.

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to ITTools. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our principles regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

1. The Core Principle: Client-Side Processing

The most important thing to know about ITTool is that we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All our tools (e.g., image compressors, text converters, password generators) are designed to be "client-side." This means all the processing and calculations happen directly on your computer, within your web browser. The files you upload and the text you paste are never sent to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

Because our tools are client-side, we do not collect any personal information you input into them. However, we do collect some non-personally identifiable information, which is standard for most websites:

  • Log Data & Analytics: Like many site operators, we use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site. This may include information such as your computer's IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our site that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages. This data is aggregated and anonymized to help us understand how our tools are being used and how to improve them.
  • Cookies: Cookies are files with a small amount of data. We may use cookies to collect information to improve our services for you. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

3. Contact Form

If you choose to contact us via our contact form, we will collect your name, email address, and the message you send. We use this information solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. We will not sell or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

5. Security

The security of your information is paramount. While our tools do not handle your data on our servers, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect our website itself. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
