Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my uploaded files secure?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. The entire conversion process happens locally in your web browser. This means your PDF files are never sent or stored on our servers, guaranteeing 100% confidentiality.

Will the HTML look exactly like my PDF?

The primary goal of this tool is to extract the text content and basic structural elements (like paragraphs and headings) into clean HTML. It may not perfectly replicate complex layouts, custom fonts, or intricate graphics. The output is designed to be a solid, editable foundation for web use, not a pixel-perfect clone.

Is there a file size limit?

Since the processing is done in your browser, performance depends on your computer's resources. For best results, we recommend using PDFs under 10MB. Larger or more complex files may take longer to process or could cause the browser to become unresponsive.
