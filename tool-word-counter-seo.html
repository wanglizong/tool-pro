Content Analysis for Modern SEO

Writing for the web in the modern era is a balance between creating engaging content for users and providing clear signals to search engines. This tool is designed to help you with both. By providing real-time feedback on key metrics, you can refine your writing on the fly.

  • Word Count: Essential for meeting content length guidelines and ensuring your articles are substantial.
  • Reading Time: An estimate of how long it will take an average person to read your text, helping you gauge user engagement.
  • Keyword Density: Shows the most frequent one- and two-word phrases in your text. This helps you check if your content is focused on your target keywords without "stuffing" them unnaturally.

Use this tool to draft articles, blog posts, or landing page copy and ensure your final text is well-structured, focused, and user-friendly.
